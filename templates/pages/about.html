About the Department

Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering (EEE) in Begum Rokeya University has commenced its academic activities on January, 2012 with three teachers and sixty students in the session of 2011-2012. The development of the present state of the society greatly depends on technological improvement. In this modern world to compete with the first world countries we need to habituate ourselves with the use of technological equipments. So opening such a department (EEE) was a notable decision. The department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering is therefore aware of proving quality education. The department provides an excellent and bright young brand of teachers and officers who are deeply committed to the university to bring out best graduates in academic disciplines and human faculty, whose services and dedications are urgently needed for the reconstruction of our society. The Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering under the faculty of Engineering and Technology offers 4 (four) years B. Sc. (Engg.) and 1 year M.Sc. Engg in Electrical and Electronic Engineering

Programs and Facilities:

A 4-year semester system based (2 semesters per year) teaching and CGPA (4.0 point scale) system of evaluation is introduced from the very first batch of students having the academic session 2011-2012.
